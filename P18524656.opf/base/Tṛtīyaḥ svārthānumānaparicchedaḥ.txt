na sādhyaḥ samudāyaḥ syāt siddhau dharmaśca kevalaḥ |sadasatpakṣabhedena śabdārthānapavādibhiḥ

vastveva cityate h yatra pratibaddhaḥ falodayaḥ |arthakriyā'samarthasya vicāraiḥ kiṃ parīkṣayā

āptavādāvisaṃvādasāmānyādanumānatā |buddheragatyābhihitā niṣiddhāpyasya gocare

moho nidānaṃ doṣāṇāmata evābhidhīyate |satkāyadṛṣṭiranyatra tatprahāṇe prahāṇataḥ

jāyate tadupādhiḥ sa śru tyā samavasīyate |tajjñānajanitajñānaḥ sa śru tāvapaṭuśrutiḥ

kartṛ saṃskārato bhinnaṃ sahitaṃ kāryabhedakṛt |sā cānupūrvī varṇānāṃ pravṛttā racanākṛtaḥ

icchāviruddhasiddhīnāṃ sthitakramavirodhataḥ |kāryakāraṇatāsiddheḥ pumbhyo varṇakramasya ca

sarvo varṇakramaḥ pubhyo dahanendhanayuktivat |asādhāraṇatā siddhā mantrākhyakramakāriṇām

puṃsāṃ jñānaprabhāvābhyāmanyeṣāṃ tadabhāvataḥ |ye'pi tantravidaḥ kecid mantrān kāṃścana kuvaṃte

prabhoḥ prabhāvasteṣāṃ sa taduktanyāyavṛttitaḥ |kṛtakāḥ pauruṣeyāśca mantrā vācyāḥ falepsunā

aśaktisādhanaṃ puṃsāmanenaiva nirākṛtam |buddhīndriyoktipuṃ stvādisādhanaṃ yattu varṇyate

atīndriyārthavit kaścidastītyabhimataṃ bhavet |svayaṃ rāgādimānnārtha vetti vedasya nānyataḥ

na vedayati vado'pi vedārthasya kuto gatiḥ |tenāgnihotraṃ juhuyāt svargakāma iti śrutau

śabdāntareṣu tādṛkṣu tādṛśyevāstu kalpanā |prasiddhiśca nṛṇāṃ vādaḥ pramāṇaṃ sa ca neṣyate

tataśca bhūyo'rthagatiḥ kimetad dviṣṭhakāmitam |atha prasiddhimullaṃdhya kalpane na nibandhanam

prasiddherapramāṇatvāt tadgrahe kiṃ nibandhanam |utpāditā prasiddhyaiva śaṃkā śabdārthaniścaye

yasmānnānārthavṛttitvaṃ śabdānāṃ tatra dṛśyate |anyathāsambhavābhavāt nānāśakteḥ svayaṃ dhvaneḥ

avaśyaṃ śaṃkayā bhāvyaṃ niyāmakamapaśyatām |eṣa sthāṇurayaṃ mārga iti vaktīti kaścana

anyaḥ svayaṃ bravīmīti tayorbhedaḥ parīkṣyatām |sarvatra yogyasyaikārthadyotane niyamaḥ kutaḥ
