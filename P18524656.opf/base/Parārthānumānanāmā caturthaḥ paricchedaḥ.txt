uttarāvayāpekṣo yo doṣaḥ so'nubadhyate |tenetyuktamato pakṣadoṣo'siddhāśrayādikaḥ

dharmidharmaviśeṣāṇāṃ svarūpasya ca dharmiṇaḥ |bādhā sādhyāṅgabhūtānāmanenaivopadarśitā

tatrodāhṛtidiṅmātramucyate'rthasya dṛṣṭaye |dravyalakṣaṇayukto'nyaḥ saṃyoge'rtho'sti dṛṣṭibhāk

adṛśyasya viśiṣṭasya pratijñā niṣprayojanā |iṣṭo hyavayavī kārya dṛṣṭvā'dṛśyeṣvasambhavi

aviśiṣṭasya cānyasya sādhane siddhasādhanam ||gurutvādhogatī syātāṃ yadyasya syāt tulānatiḥ

tannirguṇakriyastasmāt samavāyi na kāraṇam |tata eva na dṛśyo'sāvadṛṣṭeḥ kāryarūpayoḥ

tadbādhānyaviśeṣasya nāntarīyakabhāvinaḥ |āsūkṣmād dravyamālāyāstolyatvādaṃśupātavat

dravyāntaragurutvasya gatirnetyaparo'bravīt |tasya krameṇaṃ saṃyukte pāṃśurāśau sakṛd yute

bhedaḥ syād gaurave tasmāt pṛthak saha ca tolite |suvarnamāṣakādīnāṃ saṃkhyāsāmyaṃ na yujyate

sarṣapādā mahārāśeruttarottaravṛddhimat |gauravaṃ kāryamālāyā yadi naivopalabhyate

ā sarṣapād gauravaṃ tu durlakṣitamanalpakam |tolyaṃ tatkāraṇaṃ kāryagauravānupalakṣaṇāt

nanvadṛṣṭoṃ'śuvat so'rtho na ca tatkāryamīkṣyate |gurutvāgativat sarvatadguṇānupalakṣaṇāt

māṣakāderanādhikyam anatiḥ sopalakṣaṇam |yathāsvamakṣeṇādṛṣṭe rūpādāvadhikādhike

abhyupāyaḥ svavāgādyabādhāyāḥ sambhavena tu |udāharaṇamapyanyadiśā gamyaṃ yathoktyā

trikālaviṣayatvāt tu kṛtyānāmatathātmakam |tathā paraṃ prati nyastaṃ sādhyaṃ neṣṭaṃ tadāpi tat

pratyayanādhikāre tu sarvāsiddhavarodhinī |yasmāt sādhyaśrutirneṣṭaṃ viśeṣamavalambate

tenāprasiddhadṛṣṭāntahetudāharaṇaṃ kṛtam |anyathā śaśaśrṛṅgādau sarvāsiddhe'pi sādhyatā

sarvasya cāprasiddhatvāt kathañcit tena na kṣamāḥ |karmādibhedopakṣepaparihāravivecane

prāgasiddhasvabhāvatvāt sādhyo'vayava ityasat |tulyā siddhāntatā te hi yenopagamalakṣaṇāḥ

samudāyasya sādhyatve'pyanyonyasya viśeṣaṇam |sādhyaṃ dvayaṃ tadā'siddhaṃ hetudṛṣṭāntalakṣaṇam

asambhavāt sādhyaśabdo dharmivṛttiryadīṣyate |śāstreṇālaṃ yathāyogaṃ loka eva pravarttatām

sādhanākhyānasāmarthyāt tadarthe sādhyatā matā |hetvādivacanairvyāpteranāśaṃkyaṃ ca sādhanam

pūrvāvadhāraṇe tena pratijñālakṣaṇābhidhā |byarthā vyaptifalā soktiḥ sāmarthyād gamyate tataḥ

viruddhateṣṭāsambandho'nupakārasahāsthitī |evaṃ sarvāṅgadoṣāṇāṃ pratijñādoṣatā bhavet

pakṣadoṣaḥ parāpekṣo neti ca pratipāditam |iṣṭāsambhavyasiddhaśca sa eva syānnirākṛtaḥ

anityatvasahetutve śabda evaṃ prakīrttayet |daṣṭāntākhyānato'nyat kimastyatrārthānudarśanam

viśeṣabhinnāmākhyāya sāmānyasyānuvartane |na tadvyāptiḥ falaṃ vā kiṃ sāmānyenānuvartane

syānnirākaraṇaṃ śabde sthitenaivetyato'bravīt |viruddhaviṣaye'nyasmin vadannāhānyatāṃ śruteḥ

sa ca bhedāpratikṣepāt sāmānyānāṃ na vidyate |vṛkṣo na śiṃśapaiveti yathā prakaraṇe kvacit

sarvaśruterekavṛttiniṣedhaḥ syānna ceyatā |so'savaḥ sarvabhedānāmatattve tadasambhavāt

jñāpyajñāpakayorbhedāt dharmiṇo hetubhāvinaḥ |asiddherjāpakatvasya dharmyasiddhaḥ svasādhane

dharmadharmivivekasya sarvabhāveṣvasiddhitaḥ |sarvatra doṣastulyaścenna saṃvṛttyā viśeṣataḥ

paramārthavicāreṣu tathābhūtāprasiddhitaḥ |tattvānyatvaṃ padārtheṣu sāṃvṛteṣu niṣidhyate

anumānānumeyārthavyahārasthitistviyam |bhedaṃ pratyayasaṃsiddhamavalambya ca kalpyate

yathāsvaṃ bhedaniṣṭheṣū pratyayeṣū vivekinaḥ |dharmī dharmāśca bhāsante vyavahārastadāśrayaḥ

vyavahāropanīto'tra sa evāśliṣṭabhedadhīḥ |sādhyaḥ sādhanatāṃ nītastenāsiddhaḥ prakāśitaḥ

bhedasāmānyayordharmabhedādaṃgāṃgitā tataḥ |yathā'nityaḥ prayatnotthaḥ pratyatnotthatayā dhvaniḥ

pakṣāṅgatve'pyabādhatvānnāsiddhirbhinnadharmiṇi |yathāśvo na viṣāṇitvādeṣa piṇḍo viṣāṇavān

sādhyakālāṅgatā vā na nivṛtterupalakṣya tat |viśeṣo'pi pratijñārtho dharmabhedānna yujyate

pakṣadharmaprabhedena sukhagrahaṇasiddhaye |hetuprakaraṇārthasya sūtrasaṃkṣepa ucyate

ayogaṃ yogamaparairatyantāyogameva ca |vyavacchinatti dharmasya nipāto vyatirecakaḥ

viśeṣaṇaviśeṣyābhyāṃ kriyayā ca sahoditaḥ |vivakṣāto'prayoge'pi sarvo'rtho'yaṃ pratīyate

vyavacchedafalaṃ vākyaṃ yataścaitro dhanurdharaḥ |pārtho dhanurdharo nīlaṃ sarojamiti vā yathā

pratiyogivyacchedastatrāpyartheṣu gamyate |tathā prasiddheḥ samārthyās vivakṣānugamād dhvaneḥ

tadayogavyavacchedād dharmī dharmaviśeṣaṇam |tadviśiṣṭatayā dharmo na niranvayadoṣabhāk

svabhāvakāryasiddhyartha dvau dvau hetuviparyayau |vivādād bhedasāmānye śeṣo vyāvṛttisādhanaḥ

na hi svabhāvādanyena vyāptirgamyasya kāraṇe |sambhavād vyabhicārasya dvidhāvṛtifalaṃ tataḥ

prayatnānantaraṃ jñānaṃ prāk sato niyamena na |tasyāvṛtyakṣaśabdeṣu sarvathā'nupayogataḥ

kadācintirapekṣasya kāryākṛtivirodhataḥ |kādācitkafalaṃ siddhaṃ talliṅgaṃ jñānamīdṛśam

etāvataiva siddhe'pi svabhāvasya pṛthak kṛtiḥ |kāryeṇa saha nirdeśe mā jñāsīt sarvamīdṛśam

vyutpattyarthā ca hetūktiruktārthānumitau kṛtā |atra prabheda ākhyātaḥ lakṣaṇaṃ tu na bhidyate

tenātra kāryaliṅgena svabhāvo'pyekadeśabhāk |sadṛśodāhṛtiścātaḥ pratyatnād vyaktijanmanaḥ

yannāntarīkā sattā yo vātmanyavibhāgavān |sa tenāvyabhicārī syādityartha tatprabhedanam

saṃyogyādiṣu yeṣvasti pratoibandho na tādṛśaḥ |na te hetava ityuktaṃ vyabhicārasya sambhavāt

sati vā pratibandhe'stu sa eva gatisādhanaḥ |niyamo hyavinābhāvo niyataśca na sādhanam

ekāntikatvaṃ vyāvṛtteravinābhāva ucyate |tacca nāpratibaddheṣu tata evānvayasthitiḥ

svātmatve hetubhāve vā siddhe hi vyatirekitā |sidhyedato'viśeṣe na vyatirekā na vānvayaḥ

adṛṣṭimātramādāya kevalaṃ vyatirekitā |ukto'naikāntikastasmādanyathā gamako bhaveta

prāṇādyabhāvo nairātmyavyāpīti vinivarttane |ātmano vinivartteta prāṇādiryadi tacca na

anyasya vinivṛttyānyavinivṛtterayogataḥ |tadātmā tatprasūtiścet natad ātmopalambhane

tasyopalabdhāvagatāvagatau ca prasidhyati |te cātyantaparokṣasya dṛṣṭyadṛṣṭī na sidhyataḥ

anyatrādṛṣṭarūpasya ghaṭādau neti vā kutaḥ |ajñātavyatirekasya vyāvṛttervyāpitā kutaḥ

prāṇādeśca kvacid dṛṣṭyā sattvāsattvaṃ pratīyate |tathātmā yadi dṛśyeta sattvāsattvaṃ pratīyate

yasya hetorabhāvena ghaṭe prāṇo dṛśyate |dehe'pi yadyasau na syād yukto dehe na sambhavaḥ

bhinne'pi kiñcit sādharmyād yadi tattvaṃ pratīyate |prameyatvād ghaṭādīnāṃ sātmatvaṃ kinna mīyate

aniṣṭeścet pramāṇaṃ hi sarveṣṭīnāṃ nibandhanam |bhāvābhāvavyavasthāṃ kaḥ kartu tena vinā prabhuḥ

smṛtīcchāyatnajaḥ prāṇanimeṣādistadudbhavaḥ |viṣayendriyacittebhyaḥ tāḥ svajātisamudbhavāḥ

anyonyapratyayāpekṣā anvayavyatirekabhāk |etāvatyābhāvo'yamanavasthānyakalpane

śrāvaṇatvena tat tulyaṃ prāṇādi vyabhicārataḥ |na tasya vyabhicāritvād vyatireke'pi cet katham

nāsādhyādeva viśleṣastasya nanvevamucyate |sādhye'nuvṛttyabhāvo'rthāt tasyānyatrāpyasau samaḥ

asādhyādeva viccheda iti sādhye'stitocyate |arthāpattyā'ta evoktamekenobhayadarśanam

īdṛgavyabhicāro'to'nanvayiṣu na sidhyati |pratiṣedhaniṣedhaśca vidhānāt kīdṛśo'paraḥ

nivṛttirnāsataḥ sādhyādasādhyeṣveva no tataḥ |neti saiva nivṛttiḥ kiṃ nivṛtterasato matā

nivṛttyabhāvastu bidhirvastubhāvo'sato'pi san |vastvabhāvastu nāstīti paśya bāndhyavijṛmbhitam

nivṛttiryadi tasminna hetorvṛttiḥ kimiṣyate |sāpi na pratiṣedho'yaṃ nivṛttiḥ kiṃ niṣidhyate

vidhānaṃ pratiṣedhaṃ ca muktvā śābdo'sti nāparaḥ |vyavahāraḥ sa cāsatsu neti prāptātra mūkatā

satāṃ ca na niṣedho'sti so'satsu ca na vidyate |jagatyanena nyāyena nañarthaḥ pralayaṃ gataḥ

deśakālaniṣedhaśced yathāsti sa niṣidhyatena thā na yathā so'sti tathāpi na niṣidhyate

tasmādāśritya śabdārtha bhāvābhāvasamāśram |abāhyāśrayamatreṣṭaṃ sarva vidhiniṣedhanam

tābhyāṃ sa dharmi sambaddhaḥ khyātyabhavo'pi tādṛśaḥ |śabdapravṛtterastīti so'pīṣṭo vyavahārabhāk

anyathā syāt padārthānāṃ vidhānapratiṣedhane |ekadharmasya sarvātmavidhānaprariṣedhanam

anānātmatayā bhede nānāvidhiniṣedhavat |ekadharmiṇyasaṃhāro vidhānapratiṣedhayoḥ

ekadharmiṇamuddiśya nānādharmasamāśrayam |vidhāvekasya tadbhājāmivānyeṣāmupekṣakam

niṣedhe tadviviktaṃ ca tadanyeṣāmapekṣakam |vyavahāramasatyārtha prakalpayati dhīryathā

taṃ tathaivāvikalpārthabhedāśrayamupāgatāḥ |anādivāsanodbhūtaṃ bādhante'rtha na laukikam

tatfalo'tatfalaścārthe bhinna ekastatastataḥ |taistairupaplavairnītaḥ sañcayāpacayairiva

atadvānapi sambandhāt kutaścidupanīyate |dṛṣṭiṃ bhedāśrayaiste'pi tasmādajñātaviplavāḥ

sattāsādhanavṛtteśca sandigdhaḥ syādasanna saḥ |asatvaṃ cābhyupagamādapramānaṃ na yujyate

asato vyatireke'pi sapakṣād vinivarttanam |sandigghaṃ tasya sandehād vipakṣād vinivarttanam

ekatra niyame siddhe sidhyatyanyanivarttanam |dvairāśye sati dṛṣṭeṣu syāddṛṣṭe'pi saṃśayaḥ

avyaktivyāpino'pyarthāḥ santi tajjātibhāvinaḥ |kvacinna niyamo'dṛṣṭyā pārthivālohalekhyavat

bhāve virodhasyādṛṣṭe kaḥ sandehaṃ nivārayet |kvacid viniyamāt ko'nyastatkāryātmatayā sa ca

narātmyādapi tenāsya sandigdhaṃ vinivartanam |astu nāma tathāpyātmā nānairātmyāt prasidhyati

yenāsau vyatirekasya nābhāvaṃ bhāvamicchati |yathā nāvyatireke'pi prāṇādirna sapakṣataḥ

sapakṣāvyatirekī ceddheturheturatonvayī |nānvyyavyatirekī cedanairātmyaṃ na sātmakam

yannāntarīyakaḥ svātmā yasya siddhaḥ pravṛttiṣu |nivarttakaḥ sa evātaḥ pravṛttau ca pravartakaḥ

nāntarīyakatā sā ca sādhanaṃ sadhapekṣate |kāryedṛṣṭiradṛṣṭiśca kāryakāraṇatā hi sā

arthāntarasya tadbhāve bhāvāniyamato'gatiḥ |abhāvāsambhavāt teṣāmabhāve nityabhāvinaḥ

kāryasvabhāvabhedānāṃ kāraṇebhyaḥ samudbhavāt |tairvinā bhavato'nyasmāt tajjaṃ rūpaṃ kathaṃ
bhavet

sāmagrīśaktibhedāddhi vastūnāṃ viśvarūpatā |sā cenna bhedikā prāptamekarūpamidaṃ jagat

bhedakābhedakatve syād vyāhatā bhinnarūpatā |ekasya nānārūpatve dve rūpe pāvaketarau

tat tasyā jananaṃ rūpamanyasya yadi saiva sā |na tasyā jananaṃ rūpaṃ tadasyāḥ sambhavet katham

tataḥ svabhāvau niyatāvanyonyaṃ hetukāryayoḥ |tasmāt svadṛṣṭāviva tad dṛṣṭe kārye'pi gamyate

ekaṃ kathamanekasmāt kledavad dugdhavāriṇaḥ |dravaśakteḥ yataḥ kledaḥ sā tvekaiva dvayorapi

bhinnābhinnaḥ kimasyātmā bhinno'tha dravatā katham |abhinnetyucyate buddhestadrūpāyā abhedataḥ

tadvad bhede'pi dahano dahanapratyayāśrayaḥ |yenāṃśenādadhad dhūmaṃ tenāṃśena tathā gatiḥ

dahanapratyayāṅgādevānyāpekṣāt samudbhavāt |dhūmo'tadvyabhicārīti tadvat kārya tathāparam

dhūmendhanavikārāṅgatāpade dahanasthiteḥ |anagnicedadhūmo'sau sa dhūmaścet sa pāvakaḥ

nāntarīyakatā jñeyā yathāsvaṃ hetvapekṣayā |svabhāvasya yathoktaṃ prāg vināśakṛtakatvayoḥ

ahetutvagatinyāyaḥ sarvo'yaṃ vyatirekiṇaḥ |abhyūhyaḥ śrāvaṇātvokteḥ kṛtāyāḥ sāmyadṛṣṭaye

hetusvabhāvanuvṛtyaivārthanivṛttivarṇanāt |sandehahetutākhyātyā dṛśye'rthe seti sūcitam

anaṅgīkṛtavastvaṃśo niṣedhaḥ sādhyate'nayā |vastunyapi tu pūrvābhyāṃ paryudāso vidhānataḥ

tatropalabhyeṣvastitvamupalabdherna cāparam |ityajñjñāpanāyaikānupākhyodāhṛtirmatā

viṣayāsattvatastra viṣayi pratiṣidhyate |jñānābhidhānasandehaṃ yathā dāhādapāvakaḥ

tathānyā nopalabhyeṣu nāstitānupalambhabhanāt |tajjñānaśabdāḥ sādhyante tadbhāvāt tannibandhanāḥ

siddho hi vyavahāro'yaṃ dṛśyādṛṣṭāvasanniti |tasyāḥ siddhāvasandigdhau tatkāyatve'pi dhīdhvanī

vidyamāne'pi viṣaye mohādatrānanubruvan |kevalaṃ siddhasādharmyāt smāryate samayaṃ paraḥ

kāryakāraṇatā yadvat sādhyate dṛṣṭyadṛṣṭitaḥ |kāryādiśabdā hi tayorvyavahārāya kalpitāḥ

kāraṇāt kāryasaṃsiddhiḥ svabhāvāntargamādiyam |hetuprebhedākhyāne na darśitodāhṛtiḥ pṛthak

ekopalambhānubhavādidaṃ nopalabhe iti |buddherupalabhe veti kalpikāyāḥ samudbhavaḥ

viśeṣo gamyate'rthānāṃ viśiṣṭādeva vedanāt |stathābhūtātmasampattirbhedadhīheturasya ca

tasmāt svato dhiyorbhedasiddhistābhyāṃ tadarthayoḥ |anyathā hyanavasthāto bhedaḥ sighyenna kasyacit

viśiṣṭarūpānubhavādanyathānyanirākriyā |tadviśiṣṭopalambho'taḥ tasyāpyanupalambhanam

tasmādanupalambho'yaṃ svayaṃ pratyakṣato gataḥ |svamātravṛttergamakastabhāvavyavasthiteḥ

anyathārthasya nāstitvaṃ gamyate'nupalambhataḥ |upalambhasya nāstitvamanyenetyanabasthitiḥ

adṛśye niścayāyogāt sthitiranyatra bādhyate |yathā'liṅgo'nyasattveṣu vikalpādirna sidhyati

aniścayafalā hyeṣā nālaṃ vyāvṛttisādhane |ādyādhikriyate hetorniścitenaiva sādhane

tasyāḥ svayaṃ prayogeṣu svarūpaṃ vā prayujyate |arthabādhanarūpaṃ vā bhāve bhāvādabhāvataḥ

anyonyabhedasiddhervā dhrū vabhāvabimāśavat |pramāṇāntarabādhād vā sāpekṣadhru vabhāvavat

hetvantarasamutthasya sannidhau niyataḥ kutaḥ |bhāvahetubhavatve kiṃ pāramparyapariśramaiḥ

nāśanaṃ janayitvānyaṃ sa hetustasya nāśakaḥ |tameva naśvaraṃ bhāvaṃ janayed yadi kiṃ bhavet

ātmopakārakaḥ kaḥ syāt tasya siddhatmanaḥ sataḥ |nātmopakārakaḥ kaḥ syāt tena yaḥ samapekṣyate

anapekṣaśca kiṃ bhāvo'tathābhūtaḥ kadācana |yathā na kṣepabhāgiṣṭaḥ sa evodbhūtanāśakaḥ

kṣaṇamapyanapekṣatve bhāvo bhāvasya neti cet |bhāvo hi sa tathā bhūto'bhāva bhāvastathā katham

ye'parāpekṣatadbhāvāstadbhāvaniyatā hi te |asambhavadvibandhā ca samagrī kāryakarmaṇi
