tajjā tatpratibhāsā va yadi dhīrvetti nāparā |ālambamānasyānyasyāpyastyavaśyamidaṃ dvayaṃ

atha notpadyate tasmānna ca tatpratibhāsinī |sā dhīrnirviṣayā prāptā sāmānyaṃ ca tadagrahe

na gṛhyat iti proktam na ca tadvastu kiñcana |tasmādarthāvabhāso'sau nānyastasyā dhiyastataḥ

siddhe pratyakṣabhāvātmavidau gṛhṇāti tān punaḥ |nādhyakṣamiti cedeṣa kuto bhedaḥ samārthayoḥ

adṛṣṭaikārthayogādeḥ saṃvido niyamo yadi |sarvathānyo na gṛhṇīyāt saṃvidbhedo'pyapoditaḥ

yeṣāṃ ca yogino'nyasya pratyakṣeṇa sukhādikam |vidanti tulyānubhavāstadvat te'pi syurāturāḥ

viṣayemdriyasampātābhāvāt teṣāṃ tadudbhavam |nodeti duḥkhamiti cet na vai duḥkhasamudbhavaḥ

duḥkhasya vedanaṃ kintu duḥkhajñānasamudbhavaḥ |na hi duḥkhādyasaṃvedyaṃ pīḍānugrahakāraṇam

bhāsamānaṃ svarūpeṇa pīḍā duḥkhaṃ svayaṃ yadā |na tadālambanaṃ jñānaṃ na tadaivaṃ prayujyate

bhinne jñānasya sarvasya tenālambanavedane |arthasārūpyamālamba ātmā vittiḥ svayaṃ sfuṭā

api cādhyakṣatā'bhāve dhiyaḥ syālliṅgato gatiḥ |taccākṣamartho dhīḥ pūrvo manaskāro'pi vā bhavet

kāryakāraṇasāmagr yāmasyāṃ sambandhi nāparam |sāmarthyādarśanāt tatra nendriyaṃ vyabhicārataḥ

tathārtho dhīmanaskārau jñānaṃ tau ca na sidhyataḥ |nāprasiddhasya liṅgatvaṃ vyaktirarthasya
cinmatā

liṅgaṃ saiva nanu jñānaṃ vyakto'rtho'nena varṇitaḥ |vyaktāvananubhūtāyāṃ tadvyaktatvāviniścayāt

athārthasyaiva kaścit sa viśeṣo vyaktiriṣyate |nānutpādavyayavato viśeṣo'rthasya kaścana

tadiṣṭau vā pratijñānaṃ kṣaṇabhaṅgaḥ prasajyate |sa ca jñāto'tha vā'jñāto bhavejjñātasya liṅgatā

yadi jñāne'paricchinne jñāto'sāviti tat kutaḥ |jñātatvenāparicchinnamapi tad gamakaṃ katham

adṛṣṭadṛṣṭayo'nyena draṣṭrā dṛṣṭā na hi kvacit |viśeṣaḥ so'nyadṛṣṭāvapyastīti syāt svadhīgatiḥ

tasmādanumitirbuddheḥ svadharmanirapekṣiṇaḥ |kevalānnārthadharmāt kaḥ svadharmaḥ svadhiyo'paraḥ

pratyakṣādhigato hetuḥ tulyāraṇajanmanaḥ |tasya bhedaḥ kuto buddhe rvyabhicāryanyajaśca saḥ

rūpādīn pañca viṣayānindriyāṇyupalambhanam |muktvā na kāryamaparaṃ tasyāḥ samupalabhyate

tatrātyakṣaṃ dvayaṃ pañcasvartheṣveko'pi nekṣyate |rūpadarśanato jāto yo'nyathā vyastasambhavaḥ

yadevamapratītaṃ talliṅgamityatilaukikam |vidyamāne'pi liṅge tāṃ tena sārdhamapaśyataḥ

kathaṃ pratītirliṅgaṃ hi nādṛṣṭasya prakāśakam |tata evāsya liṅgāt prāk prasiddherupavarṇane

dṛṣṭāntāntarasādhyatvaṃ tasyāpītyanavasthitiḥ |ityarthasya dhiyaḥ siddhiḥ nārthāt tasyāḥ kathañcana

tadaprasiddhāvarthasya svayamevāprasiddhitaḥ |pratyakṣāṃ ca dhiyaṃ dṛṣṭvā tasyāśceṣṭābhidhādikam

paracittānumānaṃ ca na syādātmanyadarśanāt |sambandhasy manobuddhavarthaliṅgāprasiddhitaḥ

prakāśitā kathaṃ vā syāt buddhirbuddh yantareṇa vaḥ |aprakāśātmanoḥ sāmyād vyaṅgyavyañjakatā
kutaḥ

viṣayasya kathaṃ vyaktiḥ prakāśe rūpasaṃkramāt |sa ca prakāśastadrūpaḥ svayameva prakāśate

tathābhyupagame buddherbuddhau buddhiḥ svavedikā |siddhānyathā tulyadharmā viṣayo'pi dhiyā saha

iti prakāśarūpā naḥ svayaṃ dhīḥ samprakāśate |anyo'syāṃ rūpasaṃkrāntyā prakāśaḥ san prakāśate

sādṛśye'pi hi dhīranyā prakāśyā na tayā matā |svayaṃ prakāśamānā'rthastadrū peṇa prakāśate

yathā pradīpayordīpaghaṭayośca tadāśrayaḥ |vyaṅ gyavyañjakabhedena vyavahāraḥ pratanyate

viṣayendriyamātreṇa na dṛṣṭamiti niścayaḥ |tasmād yato'yaṃ tasyāpi vācyamanyasya daśanam

smṛterapyātmavit siddhā jñānasyā'nyena vedane |dīrghādigrahaṇaṃ na syād bahumātrānavasthiteḥ

avasthitāvakramāyāṃ sakṛdābhāsanānmatau |varṇaḥ syādkramo'dīrghaḥ kramavānakramāṃ katham

upakuryādasaṃśliṣyan varṇabhāgaḥ parasparam |āntyaṃ pūrvasthitādūrdhva vardhamāno dhvanirbhavet

akrameṇa grahādante kramavaddhīśca no bhavet |dhiyaḥ svayaṃ ca na sthānaṃ tadūrdhvaviṣayāsthiteḥ

sthāne svayaṃ na naśyet sā paścādapyaviśeṣataḥ |doṣo'yaṃ sakṛdutpannākramavarṇasthitāvapi

sakṛdyatnodbhavād vyarthaḥ syād yatnaścottarottaraḥ |vyaktāvapyeṣa varṇānāṃ doṣaḥ samanuṣajyate

anekayā tadgrahaṇe yāntyā dhīḥ sānubhūyate |na dīrghagrāhikā sā ca tanna syād dīrghadhīsmṛtiḥ

pṛthak pṛthak ca buddhīnāṃ saṃvittau taddhvaniśruteḥ |avicchinnābhatā na syād ghaṭanaṃ ca nirākṛtam

vicchinnaṃ śṛṇvato'pyasya yadyavicchinnavibhramaḥ |hrasvadvayoccāraṇe'pi syādavicchinnavibhramaḥ

vicchinne darśane cākṣādavicchinnādhiropaṇam |nākṣāt sarvākṣabuddhīnāṃ vitathatvaprasaṅgataḥ

sarvāntyo'pi hi varṇātmā nimeṣatulitasthitiḥ |sa ca kramādanekāṇusambandhena nitiṣṭhati

ekāṇvatyayakālaśca kālo'lpīyān kṣaṇo mataḥ |buddhiśca kṣaṇikā tasmāt kramād varṇān prapadyate

iti varṇe'pi rupādāvavicchinnāvabhāsinī |vicchinnāpyanyathā buddhiḥ sarvā syād vitathārthikā

ghaṭanaṃ yacca bhāvānāmanyatrendriyavibhramāt |bhedālakṣaṇavibhrāntaṃ smaraṇaṃ tad vikalpakam

tasya spaṣṭāvabhāsitvaṃ jalpasaṃsargiṇaḥ kutaḥ |nākṣagrāhye'sti śabdānāṃ yojaneti vivecitam

vicchinnaṃ paśyato'pyakṣairghaṭayed yadi kalpanā |arthasya tatsaṃvitteśca satataṃ bhāsamānayoḥ

bādhake sati sannyāye vicchinna iti tat kutaḥ |buddhīnāṃ śaktiniyamāditi cet sa kuto bhataḥ

yugapad buddhyadṛṣṭeścet tadevedaṃ vicāryate |tāsāṃ samānajātīye sāmarthyaniyamo bhavet

tathā hi samyaglakṣyante vikalpāḥ kramabhāvinaḥ |etena yaḥ samakṣe'rthe pratyabhiġyānakalpanām

spaṣṭāvabhāsāṃ pratyakṣāṃ kalpayet so'pi vāritaḥ |keśagolakadīpādāvapi spaṣṭāvabhāsanāt

pratītabhede'pyadhyakṣā dhīḥ kathaṃ tādṛśī bhavet |tasmānna pratyabhijñānād varṇādyekatvaniścayaḥ

pūrvānubhūtasmaraṇāt taddharmāropaṇād vinā |sa evāyamiti jñānaṃ nāsti tacchakṣaje kutaḥ

na cārthajñānasaṃvittyoryugapat sambhavo yataḥ |lakṣyete pratibhāso dau nārthārthajñānayoḥ pṛthak

na hyarthābhāsi ca jñānamartho bāhyaśca kevalaḥ |ekākāramatigrāhye bhedabhāvaprasaṅgataḥ

sūpalakṣeṇa bhedena yau saṃvittau na lakṣitau |arthārthapratyayo paścāt smaryete tau pṛthak
katham

krameṇānubhavotpāde'pyarthārthamanasorayam |pratibhāsasya nānātvacodyadoṣo duruddharaḥ

arthasaṃvedanaṃ tāvat tato'rthābhāsavedanam |na hi saṃvedanaṃ śuddhaṃ bhavedarthasya vedanam

tathā hi nīlādyākāra eka ekaṃ ca vedanam |lakṣyate na tu nīlābhe vedane vedanaṃ param

jñānāntareṇānubhavo bhavet tatrāpi hi smṛtiḥ |dṛṣṭā tadvedanaṃ kena tasyāpyanyena ced imām

mālāṃ jñānavidāṃ ko'yaṃ janayatyanubandhinīm |pūrvā dhīḥ saiva cenna syāt sañcāro viṣayāntare

tāṃ grāhyalakṣaṇaprāptāmāsannāṃ janikāṃ dhiyam |agṛhītvottaraṃ jñānaṃ gṛhṇīyādaparaṃ katham

ātmani jñānajanane svabhāve niyatāṃ ca tām |ko nāmānyo vibadhnīyād bahiraṃge'ntaraṅgikām

bāhyāḥ sannihito'pyarthastāṃ vibadhnan hi na prabhuḥ |dhiyaṃ nānubhavet kaścidanyathārthasya
sannidhau

na cāsannihitārthāsti daśā kācidato dhiyaḥ |utkhātamūlā smṛtirapyutsannetyujjvalaṃ matam

atītādivikalpānāṃ yeṣāṃ nārthasya sannidhiḥ |sañcārakaraṇābhāvād utsīdedathacintanam

ātmavijñānajanane śaktisaṃkṣayataḥ śanaiḥ |viṣayāntarasañcāro yadi saivārthadhīḥ kutaḥ

śaktikṣaye pūrvādhiyo na hi dhīḥ prāgdhiyā vinā |anyārthāsaktiviguṇe jñāne jñānodayāgateḥ

sakṛdvijātīyajātāvapyekena paṭīyasā |cittenāhitavaiguṇyādālāyānnānyasambhavaḥ

nāpekṣetānyathā sāmyaṃ manovṛttermano'ntaram |manojñānakramotpattirapyapekṣā prasādhanī

ekatvānmanaso'nyammin saktasyānyāgateryadi |jñānāntarasyānudayo na kadācit sahodayāt

samavṛttau ca tulyatvāt sarvadānyāgatirbhavet |janma vātmamanoyogamātrajānāṃ sakṛd bhavet

ekaiva cet kriyaikaḥ syāt kiṃ dīpo'nekadarśanaḥ |krameṇāpi na śaktaṃ syāt paścādapyaviśeṣataḥ

anena dehapuruṣābuktau saṃskārato yadi |niyamaḥ sa kutaḥ paścāt buddheścedastu sammatam

na grāhyatānyā jananājjananaṃ grāhyalakṣaṇam |agrāhyaṃ na hi tejo'sti na ca saukṣamyādyanaṃśake

grāhyatāśaktihāniī syāt nānyasya jananātmanaḥ |grāhyātāyā na khalvanyajjananaṃ grāhyalakṣaṇe

sākṣānna hyanyathā buddhe rūpādirupakārakaḥ |grāhyātālakṣanādanyastabhāvaniyamo'sya kaḥ

buddherapi tadastīti sāpi sattve vyavsthitā |grāhyupādānasaṃvittī cetaso grāhyalakṣaṇam

rūpādeścetasaścaivamviśuddhadhiyaṃ prati |grāhyalakṣaṇacinteyamacintyā yogināṃ gatiḥ

tatra sūkṣmādibhāvena grāhyamagrāhyatāṃ vrajet |rūpādi buddheḥ kiṃ jātaṃ paścād yat prāṅ na
vidyate

sati svadhīgrahe tasmād yaivānantarahetutā |cetaso grāhyatā saiva tato nārthāntare gatiḥ

nānekaśaktyabhāve'pi bhāvo nānekakāryakṛt |prakṛtyaiveti gaditam nānekasmānna ced bhavet

na kiñcidekasmāt sāmagrayāḥ sarvasambhavaḥ |ekaṃ syādapi sāmagryorityuktaṃ tadanekakṛt

artha pūrvañca vijñānaṃ gṛhṇīyad yadi dhīḥ parā |abhilāpadvayaṃ nityaṃ syād dṛṣṭakramamakramam

pūrvāparārthabhāsitvāccintādāvekacetasi |dvirdvirekaṃ ca bhāseta bhāsanādātmataddhiyoḥ

viṣayāntarasañcāre yadyantyaṃ nānubhūyate |parānubhūtavat sarvānanubhūtiḥ prasajyate

ātmānubhūta pratyakṣaṃ nānubhūtaṃ parairyadi |ātmānubhūtiḥ sā siddhā kuto yenaivamucyate

vyaktihetvaprasiddhiḥ syāt na vyaktervyaktamicchataḥ |vyaktyasiddhavapi vyaktaṃ yadi vyaktamidaṃ
jagat
